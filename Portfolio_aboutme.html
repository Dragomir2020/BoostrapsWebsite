About Me

Hello I'm a Freshman engineering student at the University of Cincinnati!! I also enjoy binge watching Netflix with my friends and staying up till 3 AM every night doing homework in the engineering building.

I am passionate about giving back to my community, research and gaining further knowledge of a concept. Thought my high school career I have been involved with Habitat for Humanity. I am passionate about this organization because it not only allows me to give back to someone who is less fortunate, it allows me to gain a skill and acquire a sense of purpose. I have also been involved in research and am extremely interested in conducting research that brings about new knowledge and helps make a difference in the scientific community. Finally, I enjoy reading other scientists findings and using their own work to help me model how I like to conduct my own research.

Into the future, I am striving to find a job which fulfills my drive to find meaning in life. I plan to pursue a PHD and eventually work towards running my own laboratory and working at a university. As of now I am currently pursuing an undergraduate degree and taking any opportunities available that will help me gain experience which can lead to my overall goals.
